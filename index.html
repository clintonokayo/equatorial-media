What Are Video Editing Services?

Video editing services involve transforming raw footage into polished, engaging videos. This includes:

  • Trimming and cutting footage
  • Adding transitions and effects
  • Background music and sound mixing
  • Text overlays and animations
  • Color correction and grading
  • Subtitles and captions
  • Exporting for YouTube, IGTV, TikTok, and more
